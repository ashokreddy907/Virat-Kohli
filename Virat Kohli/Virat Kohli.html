About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world and holds numerous cricket records. Kohli has achieved great success in international cricket and has received several awards and accolades for his performances. He is known for his aggressive playing style and his ability to chase high targets. Kohli is also involved in various philanthropic activities and is considered a role model for aspiring cricketers.
